About Me

Born and raised in Halifax I always knew I wanted a fun job where I could express my creativity and flaunt my organizational skills. I decided to go to Culinary school to become a Pastry Chef. My career as a Pastry Chef has taken me all over the world; from Ottawa to Germany, Vancouver to back home here in Halifax. I've had a great career and many wonderful experiences along the way.

Unfortunately working in kitchens is no longer a viable career option, so my focus has turned to tech. A career in Tech will allow me to retain my creative edge while giving me a wider platform to show the world what I can do.
