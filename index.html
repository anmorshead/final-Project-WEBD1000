About Me: Andrea Morshead



Growing up in Halifax, I always knew I wanted a job that was not only fun but also let me express my creativity and flaunt my organizational skills. So, I decided to attend Culinary school with the dream of becoming a Pastry Chef. My journey in the world of pastries has taken me on an incredible adventure, from Ottawa to Germany, Vancouver, and back home here in Halifax. It's been a blast, filled with amazing experiences! However, the kitchen life has lost its appeal as a long-term career option for me. So, I've set my sights on the tech world. A career in Tech seems like the perfect fit—it allows me to keep my creative juices flowing while offering a much broader stage to showcase what I can do. Exciting times ahead!
